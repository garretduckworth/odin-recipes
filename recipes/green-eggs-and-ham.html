Green Eggs and Ham

Ham with Green Eggs

  • 8 slices of ham
  • 8 Eggs
  • 2 cups of spinach
  • Salt and Pepper

STEPS:

  1. Heat skillet
  2. Microwave Ham and leave
  3. Place cracked eggs and spinach into a blender utill smooth
  4. Pour mixture into pan
  5. cook until done
  6. Enjoy with your favorite book!
